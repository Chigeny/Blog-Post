Tips to Boost Your Self Love
And Heal From Emotional Abuse.

Here are tips to help you get over that heart break.
Dont waste your time thinking.
Learn these tips, affirm them, and become your best version.

  1. Only God completes you. You are a support to another human.
  2. Don't make decisions in your despiration to heal.
    Deal with it, process it and heal from it.
  3. Let God give you a release that this is right for you.
    God will never give us something that is bad
    His plans are always good for us. Pray to God before deciding on marriage
  4. Don't ignore red flags and don't get stuck in it even when you are already married.
  5. Don't ignore traces and patterns from someone else.
  6. Don't endure abuse for the sake of children. It also affects them.
  7. Know yourself. Know who you are and identify your purpose.
    For a union, purposes have to align.
  8. Love yourself enough to know that no human being should have power over you.
  9. No human has the right to control your emotions, your psychological state.
  10. Affirm positivity on yourself daily. Appreciate yourself.
    Don't seek validation from others. Hype yourself.
  11. You cannot accept a name that is not yours, don't let it hurt you.
  12. Don't force things. any relationship that is not working, let it go.
    I am enough. Appreciate all that God has put in you.
  13. Learn to thank God every day. Everything I have now is what I need.
    Don't focus on your wants.
  14. Be selfish with your peace of mind.
  15. God is your husband, He can do all things and more for you.
  16. Learn and choose to believe in your self.
